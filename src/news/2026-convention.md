We're returning to Granite Bay for our yearly convention. Come join us for a day of community - anyone interested in NATRC is welcome. The 2025 year-end awards will be presented, and we'll look ahead to the 2026 season.

Our speakers will be Adrienne Hendricks, a professional saddle maker and the official Tevis saddle outfitter, and Angie Meroshnekoff, who has 40+ years of experience as a competitor, judge, ride manager, and mentor.
